About Maketronics

With offices in the UAE, and India. We are a team of experts who help procurement, engineering, and design teams navigate through the complex landscape of sourcing electronic components. We extend value to our customers ranging from Automotive, Healthcare, Defense, Logistics, Retail Electronics, Logistics, iOT, Oil & Gas and Telecommunications by sourcing obsolete, hard-to-find, discontinued, and end-of-life electronic components. We pride ourselves on our exemplary customer service, sense of urgency, ability to build trust and provide convenience to our customers.

Our Vision

To become a top 10 global distributor of obsolete electronic components by 2030, recognized for our ability to manage component obsolescence, our commitment to quality, and our innovative solutions that address the evolving needs of our customers.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality electronic and electrical components, with a focus on obsolete parts, ensuring customer satisfaction through a commitment to excellence, compliance with international standards, and a customer-centric approach.
